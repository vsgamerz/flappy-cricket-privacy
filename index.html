Privacy Policy — Flappy Cricket

Effective date: 19 July 2026

Flappy Cricket ("the app") is a mobile game developed by Vivek Sinha ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information is handled when you play the app.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information ourselves. The app does not require an account, and your scores and settings (best score, sound and replay preferences) are stored only on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes them.

Advertising (third-party services)

The app shows advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain data to provide and measure ads, including your device's advertising identifier, IP address, coarse location derived from IP, device information, and ad interaction data. This collection is performed by Google under its own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data in AdMob

Where required by law (for example in the EEA/UK), you will be asked for consent before personalised ads are shown, and you can change your choice at any time from within the app. You can also reset or delete your advertising ID in your device's Google settings.

App permissions

  • Internet / network access — used only to load advertisements.
  • Vibration — used for gameplay haptic feedback. No data is involved.

Children

The app is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app's advertising, please contact us and we will work with our ad provider to address it.

Data retention and deletion

We hold no user data on any server. All app data lives on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app. For data handled by Google AdMob, refer to Google's policies linked above.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
